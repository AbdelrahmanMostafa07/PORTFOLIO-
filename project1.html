Project 1

Project Goals

The aim of my project is to explore and understand customer behavior patterns during the purchasing process.

I seek to identify the factors influencing product choices and customer interactions with services.

Additionally, I aim to establish connections between different variables such as age and gender to gain a better understanding of these dynamics.

Project Steps

Define questions and hypotheses

1- Is there a distinct pattern in age groups regarding purchasing behavior?

2- Are there gender-based differences in product preferences?

3- Does gender influence the amount spent on purchases?

4- Does the season affect the types of products purchased?

5- Is there a preferred payment method among older age groups?

Data Cleaning:

Examine the data to identify and correct any errors or missing values.

Take actions to enhance data quality and ensure the accuracy of the analysis.

Data Analysis:

Once the data has been thoroughly cleaned and prepared, the next step is to conduct a comprehensive analysis.

Utilizing statistical methods and data visualization tools, explore the dataset to derive meaningful insights.
